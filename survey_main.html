Policy Survey

Health at a Glance: Latin America and the Caribbean

Thematic chapter on quality-of-care policies

This questionnaire is designed to collect information from LAC countries on quality-of-care policies and overall government efforts to assure and improve the quality of healthcare. The work based on this information will be released in 2026 as a thematic chapter of the third edition of Health at a Glance: Latin America and the Caribbean, co-published by the OECD, the World Bank and the Inter-American Development Bank.

The questionnaire has two parts. Part I maps the state of play in quality-of-care policies—what exists in law and governance, the standards that guide practice, how performance is measured and improved, and how patients and professionals are engaged. Part II asks about the digital health capabilities and the uses of health data for quality improvement.
